Harbin

Gothic Architecture

Harbin Sacred Heart of Jesus is located in the centre of Harbin City. The predecessor of the church is the St Stanislav Church. The church was a Christianity church built by Polish foreign residents. The church is a typical Gothic architecture.

The Capital of Ice and Snow

Harbin is now a large, open city, influenced deeply by Russia, Europe, and Japan in its culture, architecture, and lifestyle. Also called "the Ice City", Harbin is one of the most popular destinations for winter tours, hosting the world's biggest ice and snow festival.

City of Music

Harbin has been dubbed the "City of Music" by UNESCO, for good reason. Having developed for a century, music has become a unique icon of the city, where eastern and western music cultures meet and interact. Harbin is the venue of the China-Harbin Summer Concert, the longest-running musical event in China.
